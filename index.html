Our Story

A home for coffee lovers

Café Mira was born in 2019 from a simple idea: good coffee should have time. We roast in small batches, work directly with farmers, and bake fresh every morning. Come in, stay a while, read a book — we're glad you're here.

  • Direct-trade coffee from Ethiopia & Colombia
  • Vegan & gluten-free options daily
  • Dog-friendly & quiet corners to work
